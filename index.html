Malware in the Wild

Although vaguely known to the public, Malware presents a substantial and growing risk to systems around the world. Every year, Malware costs private individuals and companies millions of dollars both in lost data and system down time. This presentation aims to educate the general public regarding the dangers of clicking a "simple link."
